What Is A Digital Wholesale Broker?

In an age where technology is touching every aspect of our personal and professional lives, algorithms can replace the traditional underwriter model and promote a real-time submission and quoting process.

Empire Digital’s technology is powered by Appulate™ and provides agents with the fastest and easiest way to rate, quote and bind risks online.

Empire Digital works with leading insurance companies to create this highly automated process which results in speed and responsiveness for our agent partners as well as a higher level of commissions.

While Empire Digital may look more like a tech company than a wholesale brokerage, our DNA is insurance. Empire Insurance has a legacy dating back more than 100 years and is operated by highly credible management with decades of insurance experience.
